WIDEPIX v0.5 beta

Made by Nash Muhandes

A collection of widescreen (up to 21:9!) 2D graphics replacements. Throw it in your autoload, enjoy widescreen images!

NOTE: DO NOT include this mod in your modpacks. Permissions will be updated at a later time when my work is declared complete.
